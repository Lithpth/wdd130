Audience

Who are our customers? Our target audience includes individuals of all ages, with a particular focus on young adults aged 18-35 who crave excitement and new challenges. What do our customers want? They want a fun and adventurous rafting experience that is safe, well-organized, and provides an opportunity for personal growth and teamwork! What can we offer to fulfill their neeeds? Additional activities and amneties such as camping or hiking options. Customers deserve a customized trip fit for them!

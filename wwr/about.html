History

After the passing of Sir Charles Quandaledingle III, it left a hole in our family. He wasn't related to us but he may as well have been. Due to his passing the only way I thought I could cope with it was to make a rafting company. He always called me "daft" so I named the company "Daft Rafts"

We've been in business for over 10 years and have been providing high quality and safe rafting experiences. Our experienced guides ensure that everyone has a great time and leaves with unforgettable memories.
